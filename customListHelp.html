Word Find

Help

In the 'Make Custom List' page, you can create new word lists by entering each word. You can switch to 'Upload File' mode by clicking the tab at the top right corner.

How to Create a Custom List:
  1. Enter a name for the word list in 'Display name.'
  2. Enter words in the 'Word List' field. One word per box. (Minimum of 5 words per puzzle.)
  3. Press the 'Save Puzzle' button to create the new list.
  • Recommended to name the puzzle something unique and descriptive.
