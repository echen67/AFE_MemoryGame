Word Find

Help

The home page lets you choose between jumping into a puzzle game or uploading new word lists.

- Create new word lists by pressing the 'Create New Word Lists' button.

To begin a game:
  1. Press 'Select Word List.'
  2. Select the word list you would like to play.
  3. Once you have selected the list you would like to play, press the 'Play' button.
  4. Enjoy your Word Find Puzzle!
